About ZERO

Zero is an AI-based security scanner that is aiming to enhance the security of ATMs and CDMs, along with banks.
When someone try to enter an ATM or a bank wearing helmets, hats, or covering their faces, Zero will instantly identify them and prevent them from entering to premises until they have removed those head wears
